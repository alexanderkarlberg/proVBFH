#+TITLE: scale dependence of DGLAP evolution

To check how much of the N3LO PDFs would come from the missing higher
order terms in the DGLAP evolution, we check the impact of the scale
variation on the evolution.

We run using different reference Q0 (5 GeV, 8 GeV, 10 GeV), and do a
variation by a factor two up and down.

All in all, effects of higher order DGLAP looks to be:
 - up to 1% at NLO
 - up to 1%o at NNLO

* values at NLO:

  | units=pb    | Q0 = 5 GeV | Q0 = 8 GeV | Q0 = 10 GeV |
  |-------------+------------+------------+-------------|
  | xmuR = 0.5  |     3.8948 |     3.8900 |      3.8882 |
  | xmuR = 1.0  |     3.8806 |     3.8820 |      3.8823 |
  | xmuR = 2.0  |     3.8800 |     3.8826 |      3.8833 |
  | 3-loop evln |     3.8767 |     3.8787 |      3.8794 |


  | change wrt central | Q0 = 5 GeV | Q0 = 8 GeV | Q0 = 10 GeV |
  |--------------------+------------+------------+-------------|
  | xmuR = 0.5         |    +0.0037 |    +0.0021 |     +0.0015 |
  | xmuR = 2.0         |    -0.0002 |    +0.0002 |     +0.0003 |
  | 3-loop evln        |    -0.0010 |    -0.0009 |     -0.0007 |

* values at NNLO
